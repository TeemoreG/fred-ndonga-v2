Our Story

We started on the floor. We know your world.

Before we became trainers or consultants, we were on the floor — clearing plates, serving tables, handling angry guests at 6 AM. That experience taught us something no textbook could: great hospitality isn't about fancy policies. It's about consistency, pride, and systems that actually work.

Over the past decade, we worked our way through Sarova Hotels — from the front lines to training new teams. We earned Trainer of Trainers certification from Kenya Utalii College and built a reputation for turning chaotic operations into smooth-running departments.

Today, we work with hotel owners, restaurant founders, and resort operators across Kenya. We don't hand you a report and disappear. We stand beside your team, fix what's broken, and teach your people to run better shifts — long after we're gone.

— Prime Consultancy
