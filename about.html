About Fred Ndonga

From the floor to the boardroom — a career built on real hospitality experience.

I started at the bottom. I know your world.

Before I became a trainer or a consultant, I was on the floor — clearing plates, serving tables, handling angry guests at 6 AM. That experience taught me something no textbook could: great hospitality isn't about fancy policies. It's about consistency, pride, and systems that actually work.

Over the past decade, I worked my way through Sarova Hotels — from the front lines to training new teams. I earned my Trainer of Trainers certification from Kenya Utalii College and built a reputation for turning chaotic operations into smooth-running departments.

Today, I work with hotel owners, restaurant founders, and resort operators across Kenya. I don't hand you a report and disappear. I stand beside your team, fix what's broken, and teach your people to run better shifts — long after I'm gone.

— Fred Ndonga
